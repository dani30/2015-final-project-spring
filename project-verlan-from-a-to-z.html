VERLAN FROM A TO Z

design: box and index cards

dimensions: 2.75" x 4.75"

A set of twenty-six cards that feature verlan (slang) words from A to Z. Verlan is a form of French argot popular among the youth of France, basically consisting of an inversion of the syllables of a word. Cards are categorized by parts of speech and include pronunciation, a French definition, an English translation, and a sentence that uses the word in context. All info is provided in both French and English.
